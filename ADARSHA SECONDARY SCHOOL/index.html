School Facilities

Adarsha Secondary School provides a well-equipped learning environment with spacious classrooms, modern computer labs, and a comprehensive e-library. We also offer dedicated spaces for co-curricular activities, ensuring a holistic development for all our students. Our commitment extends to providing a fully accessible and disabled-friendly infrastructure.

School Bus

Safe and reliable transportation is available for students, ensuring convenient access to and from school.

Playground

Our spacious playground provides a safe and engaging area for students to play, exercise, and develop teamwork.

Healthy Canteen

We prioritize the well-being of our students by offering a canteen that serves nutritious and healthy food options.

Positive Learning

Adarsha fosters a supportive, inclusive, and encouraging atmosphere where students feel motivated to learn and grow.

Principal's Message

Dear Students, Parents, Guardians, and Well-wishers,

Welcome to Adarsha Secondary School, Biratnagar! For over 95 years, we've been a cornerstone of education here, committed to nurturing bright futures.

We offer quality Plus Two programs (NEB) in Science, Education, and Management, as well as technical courses (CTEVT). We pride ourselves on providing holistic education, excellent teaching, and a supportive environment for all students, including being the only disabled-friendly community school in Biratnagar.

Explore our website to learn more about our programs, facilities, and vibrant school life. We value the partnership between home and school and look forward to working together to help our students succeed.

Santosh Pokhrel
Principal
